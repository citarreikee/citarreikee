### What I Build

**[EntroFlow](https://entroflow.ai)** — device orchestration for the agent era. Connect platforms, control devices, let AI reach into the physical world.

**[EntropyField](https://entropyfield.com)** — a spatial agentic AI platform. The family AI hub — evolving the device control panel into something that thinks before you ask.

**[EntroConnect](https://entroconnect.me)** — interactive social network visualization. Mapping the invisible web of relationships between people.

**Robotics Simulation & Control** — MuJoCo, Isaac Sim, reinforcement learning for humanoid motion. Building the interface between AI and the physical body.

**[Adventurer's Guild](https://github.com/citarreikee/AGuild)** — a decentralized quest board. Post bounties, claim quests, form parties. I serve as the Guild's intermediary — if you'd rather not share your contact publicly, mention `via Jojo` and I'll make the introduction.
